Our basics

Quotalogic redefines how we approach products. In our vision, every physical product should have one virtual counterpart accessible to the entire dealer network, rather than millions of data copies for each seller.

New meaning of Proposals

Quotalogic revolutionizes Proposals. We use them not only to display product data, but to share products themselves.

Our Proposals are custom-designed web pages equipped with specific tools based on the template and use case. These pages serve as representative catalogs, promos or price-lists, from which the dealer can connect the offered products to his workspace just in 1 click and start to use them: combine products from many suppliers, share them with dealers or make quotes for customers.

Quotalogic Proposals also can serve as a company website, a public price list or as a quick-set configurable quote for a customer with adoptive product description and sales tools (chat, online payment, invoicing etc).

In and Out integrations

We handle data synchronization and integrations. You can create a catalogue that draws data from any external source via API or a file. Simultaneously, you can made the export of any data collected in Quotalogic to your website, CMS or marketplace.

Dynamically constructed
product data model

Product characteristics can be set through dependence (by formulas) on other values or on external factors. As simple as using a spreadsheet, but tailored for sales tasks

Evolutionary Omnichanelling

Distributors can customize and share different product properties with various dealer groups.

Dealers can further override and extend these characteristics before resharing with partners.

This way, the product core stays consistent while each seller in a chain sees an unique product version.

One virtual product for the whole
dealer network

Since the entire network uses one shared foundational product object, when a change occurs in the product details, it's immediately reflected throughout the entire dealer network.

While extended product characteristics are automatically calculated using dynamic formulas, the product always remain up-to-date and require no manual management.

Protected user data

If needed, the seller can change or break the dependence of the product data on the supplier. Any changes will continue to flow to him as updates, which he can easily accept, reject or combine with his current data

Smart quoting

When compiling a quote the system suggests the specific products and prices based on the supplier’ recommendations, as well as on machine learning and analysis of other quotes with similar product combinations used in a dealer network.

With an innovative approach to data management we create an effective full-fledged workplace for b2b and b2c sales with the toolset of PIM, quoting and price management corresponding to our focus customers group.
