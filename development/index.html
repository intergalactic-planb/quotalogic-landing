Quotalogic

Streamlined eCommerce:
Shared Catalogs & Automated Offers
Sharing products from many suppliers as easy as sharing music tracks from many artists on cloud services. Combine them into ‘playlist', tune it, use it or share with partners.

Features

Catalog sharing for wide dealers networks

  • The fastest way to give products to the supply chain
  • Actual data and effective sales tools for dealers

An instant access to multiple suppliers catalogs

  • Take and use any products of many suppliers directly from their catalogues or even from promos, pricelists and quotations
  • Combine and re-share product sets with partners and customers by simple drag-n-drop

A fastest and easiest quotation tool

  • Full and always actual multiple suppliers’ product data.
  • AI-automated compilation of quotes.
  • No CRM and PIM deployment. No API needed, no coding, no file-parsing, no set-up: just choose-and-sell

Easy start

Connect an existing data source, upload a file or create your own product set manually.
Join a ready-to-use catalog of your supplier in one click. Forget the worries about data accuracy and currency.
Tune product data with the built-in CMS. Receive data updates from any external source or use always actual supplier’s catalogs.

Smart product sets sharing

Forget about files and data parsing.
Share your product selections as an interactive catalog tuned to the needs of a specific partner. Join it to a new workspace in one click and immediately start to use products.

Share promotions, sales pitches and key points, unique selling propositions and experts chat channel attached to the product.

Multiple suppliers catalogs usage

Combine new sets of products using many suppliers catalogs, price lists, promotions, commercial proposals, – and share them with your partners.

The dynamic data model allows you to forget about the need to update products: they will be continuously updated by the supplier.

Business proposals generation has never been faster and easier.
